Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you begin.

The website helps you prepare content and demonstrate flows. It does not send automated messages by itself.

Is this an official WhatsApp or Meta product?
No. It is an independent template and flow-planning tool for real estate professionals and is not affiliated with or endorsed by WhatsApp or Meta.
Does the website automatically message my leads?
No. It creates messages and demonstrates conversation logic in your browser. Actual automation requires a compliant WhatsApp Business platform or human sending process.
Where are my saved drafts stored?
Drafts are stored in your current browser using localStorage. They do not automatically appear on another browser or device, so JSON export is recommended for backups.
Can I customize the templates?
Yes. The planned Template Maker supports business, customer, property, lead and consultant details alongside editable messages and dynamic variables.
Can I create complete conditional flows?
The Flow Builder will let you arrange messages, replies, conditions, delays, CRM tags and handovers for browser-based preview and export.
Should customers send documents through these templates?
Sensitive documents should only be collected through a secure verified system or an approved human process. Never request confidential documents through an unverified link.
