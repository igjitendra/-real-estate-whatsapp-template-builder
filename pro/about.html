About

Better property conversations without unnecessary complexity.

A practical browser toolkit for professional real estate WhatsApp messages and chat flows.

Why this project exists

Real estate conversations often begin with an unstructured message. This browser-based toolkit helps agents, brokers, consultants, builders and sales teams create consistent, respectful conversations without complicated automation software.
