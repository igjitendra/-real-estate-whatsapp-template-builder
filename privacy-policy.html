Privacy overview

Effective 24 July 2026

This static application works primarily in your browser. It does not require an account and does not automatically transmit template fields, draft contents or simulator inputs to a project-owned server.

Information stored locally

Browser storage may hold saved drafts, favourites, saved flows, autosave state and up to five recent logic simulations. Clearing drafts or site data removes this information.

Exports and imports

TXT and JSON files are created or read only after a user action. You control where exported files are stored and shared.

External services

User-triggered actions may open WhatsApp, Instagram or other websites, which apply their own privacy policies. Google-hosted font files may be requested when internet access is available.

Sensitive information

Do not enter identity documents, banking credentials, card details, secrets or unnecessary customer information.

Changes

This notice may change with the product. The effective date identifies the current version.
